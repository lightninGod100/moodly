Moodly (Mood Tracker App)
Problem Statement
People often struggle to track their emotional well-being and identify patterns in their mood over time. There's also a lack of connection to how others are feeling globally, which can lead to feelings of isolation.
Moodly solves this by letting users:
Track daily mood changes
View mood history and trends
See a real-time world mood map
Receive uplifting support when feeling low
Moodly promotes self-awareness, emotional insight, and a sense of global connection — all in one app.

Functionality
User Auth
Mood selection
Personal Mood Stats
Global Mood stats
Message Card from other users
